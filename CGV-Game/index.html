Echoes of the Fallen

In a time long forgotten, the world wasn’t a mere battleground of endless waves. It was alive—each corner of its forests, deserts, and cities was filled with life and wonder. But darkness came, corrupting the heart of this land and its inhabitants. Once-peaceful creatures now rise as relentless enemies, drawn to the energy of a mysterious Wanderer… you.

You are the last defender, bound by an ancient call to cleanse this land. As you traverse from the twisted forests to the barren deserts and finally to the forsaken ruins of the city, waves of darkened enemies challenge you, testing your strength and resolve.

Every enemy you vanquish pushes back the corruption, but the darkness is stubborn. After a seemingly endless onslaught, each area releases its final protector—a powerful, corrupted entity, an embodiment of the darkness. In the depths of the forest, at the heart of the desert, and within the shadows of the city, these bosses lie in wait.

As each boss falls, the land briefly clears, and a part of this world’s spirit is restored. Yet, the battle is far from over. Will you overcome every foe and bring light back to this shattered world, one stage at a time? Or will you fall to the relentless forces of darkness that have plagued this land for so long?

Prepare yourself, Wanderer. The journey ahead is filled with enemies, ancient guardians of the shadows, and the promise of victory… if you have the strength to claim it.
